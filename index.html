Bleu Collar Sandwiches: Food For Real Workers.

  • Real Ingredients

    Bleu Collar Sandwiches sources meat, cheese, produce, and bread from local farmers and bakeries, so we always offer the freshest grub with no preservatives, artificial colors, or processed ingredients. Like you, we're handy: all of our condiments and special sauces are home-made.

  • Plenty of Protein

    We know it takes energy to keep this country running. That's why our standard protein portions are equivalent to one full serving. Want more meat? Just double up on protein for a thicker bite.

  • Just How You Like It

    We don't compromise on our ingredients and neither should you. We strive to make your sandwich exactly how you like it, so let us know of any special requests and you'll get your meal your way.

More About Us

Bleu Collar Sandwiches was started by Jim Havarti and Colleen McGuckin in the back of a Jeep Cherokee. We started out servicing industrial parks in the Northwest suburbs of Chicago after forging relationships with local suppliers. With nation-wide connections to bakeries and farmers, Bleu Collar upgraded to a Chevy Econo Van and is now touring the U.S.A.
